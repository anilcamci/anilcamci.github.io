In(tensive) C(are): Terry Riley in AlarmVR
In this project, PAT BFA student Spencer Harris worked with me on leveraging the AlarmVR project to develop a whole new musical system. As a case study of this system, he configured AlarmVR for an interactive rendition of Terry Riley’s seminal piece “In C” using virtual medical monitoring devices. The custom synthesizer that he designed in PureData allows the user to modify the sounds and behaviors of the alarms emanating from these devices in real time as they play sequences from “In C” in binaural audio.
