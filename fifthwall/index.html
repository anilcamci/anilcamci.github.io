Brining Down the Fifth Wall:
A System for Delivering VR Performances to Large Audiences
This project deals with the design of an intelligent cinematography system for VR performances, where the real-time analysis of the performed music and the behavior of the performer dictate the procedural generation and control of a camera system that offers engaging and immersive representations of the performance to the concert audience.
