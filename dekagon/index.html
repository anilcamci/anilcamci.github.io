Dekagon: An Album of Electronic Music
Ten works of electronic music from a ten-year period: an ode to synthesis. Dekagon is a journey into sonic worldmaking with each piece leveraging a different synthesis tool or technique from custom designs to timeless classics. As spectrum, dynamics, space, and time become continuous dimensions of musical articulation, Dekagon moves the listener between the physical reality of their listening environment and an implied universe grounded in sound. This album is at the center of my book “The Cognitive Continuum of Electronic Music,” which was published by Bloomsbury in 2022. Dekagon was selected as one of the eight albums to be produced as Innova Recording's first national call.
