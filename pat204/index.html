PAT 204: Creative Coding for Music
In PAT 204 Creative Coding for Music, students learn about fundamental concepts of software programming and apply these to a range of artistic projects. In this video, we see examples from this year’s showcase, where students presented their final projects. These projects ranged from live audiovisual performances, to interactive installations and multiplayer games.
