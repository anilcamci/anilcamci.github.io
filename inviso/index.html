Inviso: Creativity Support Tool for Designing Immersive Sonic Environments
Inviso is a web-based application for designing and experiencing rich and dynamic sonic virtual realities. It enables both novice and expert users to construct complex immersive sonic environments with 3D sound components. In this video, we see some of the core functionality of Inviso, including omni-directional sound objects, uni-directional sound cones, non-directional sound zones, and motion trajectories. After creating an environment, the user can export it to share it with others or to revisit it later. An updated version of Inviso with multi-user functionality and cross-reality operation, is showcased here. Crowdscapes, a crowd-sourced soundscape installation based on Inviso, was awarded the Best Installation Prize at the New Interfaces for Musical Expression (NIME) Conference in 2020.
