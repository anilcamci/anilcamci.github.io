Anıl Çamcı
Vylderness:
Worldmaking through Modular Synthesis and VR
In this structured improvisation for modular synthesizer and virtual reality, Çamcı explores an audiovisual continuum that stretches across abstract and representational structures, leveraging a real-time network between a hardware synthesizer and a game engine. As Çamcı constructs synthesized sounds into musical structures, a virtual world begins to take shape with elements that react to the performer's interactions with the synthesizer and the resulting sounds. Thereby, the virtual environment becomes an extension of the instrument, revealing an audiovisual narrative that unfolds over the course of the performance.
