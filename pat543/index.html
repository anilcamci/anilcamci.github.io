PAT 443/543: Immersive Media
In this course, we explore modern immersive media technologies and their applications in performing arts. Throughout the semester, students develop critical perspectives on the histories and aesthetics of immersive media and gain hands-on experience with VR systems, game engines, and spatial audio. In this video, we see segments from this year’s public showcase of student projects, which featured virtual reality music instruments, interactive VR concert experiences, VR games, and immersive sonic environments.
