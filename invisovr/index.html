A System for Designing Immersive Audio in VR
In this project, PAT BS student Aaron Willette worked with me on creating a Virtual Reality version of my browser-based immersive audio design tool Inviso. Working among a team of undergraduate researchers, Aaron explored how fundamental elements of spatial audio design could be brought into a VR context. Aaron’s work on this system was featured in a New Interfaces for Musical Expression paper in 2020.
