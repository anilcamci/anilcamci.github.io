Crowdscapes: A Crowd-sourced Soundscape Installation
As we adjusted to social distancing practices due to the COVID-19 pandemic, our relationship with our surroundings changed in fundamental ways. While certain aspects of our daily routines moved out of focus, others became magnified. Our everyday environments remained familiar yet they were stripped of their social dimension. Crowdscapes brought these experiences into a shared space in the form of iterative and branching virtual sonic environments. Using the browser-based immersive audio tool Inviso, the participants reflected upon their experiences through sound and virtual movement. Each environment became an assemblage of solitary experiences accumulating into crowd-sourced soundscapes. This work was awarded the Best Installation Prize the 2020 New Interfaces for Musical Expression Conference.
