Creative Coding Collective
Creative Coding Collective explores the integration of multimedia technology in collaborative music performance and interactive art. They create original works that position technology not only as a performance medium but also as a conceptual catalyst for system design as an art form. Drawing from systems theory and cybernetics, the collective aims to understand and advance the role of technology in co-creative practices. I reflect on my role as the director of this ensemble in the upcoming Association for Technology in Music Instruction Conference.
